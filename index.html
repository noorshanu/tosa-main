HOW IT WORKS?

A “Tosa Fuse” is a crypto wallet that holds at least 1,000,000 $Tosa tokens.

To vote, a Tosa Fuse simply selects which Contender they are voting for on the Tosa dApp, before the Bomb wallet hits $10,000.

Each Tosa Fuse gets 1 vote, per Tosa Bomb, plus any additional votes from holding Tosa NFTs. Each Fuse’s vote is public information on the Tosa dApp.

If a Fuse fails to vote in the current Bomb round, their vote(s) do not carry over to the following round. Once vote(s) are submitted, they cannot be withdrawn.
